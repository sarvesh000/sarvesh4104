About Us

We have been in the textile business since 1960's and Harmony Ekma Cotfabs Private Limited is a Private company incorporated on 17 February 2009.

Company's directors are Omprakash Chhaparwal, Krishnagopal Chhaparwal, Harish Chhaparwal and Rajgopal Chhaparwal.

Company's expertise lies in Uniform fabrics, pocketing fabrics, Cotton drills, printed suiting fabrics, and 100% cotton shirtings. Fabric is manufactured at Ichalkaranji and marketed pan India. Quality Assurance has been a fundamental and non-negotiable part for Us. Our guiding philosophy has been to provide our clients the best quality at competitive market price.
